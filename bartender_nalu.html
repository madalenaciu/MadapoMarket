Bartender at Nalu

Location: Leoforos Poseidonos, Alimos 174 55

Job Type: Full-time, From 6am till closing(usually 2pm)

About Us:Nalu is a vibrant and well-established /restaurant/cafe known for its exceptional drinks, inviting atmosphere, and excellent customer service. We are seeking a skilled and passionate bartender to join our team and contribute to our mission of providing memorable experiences for our guests.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and serve a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages with precision and creativity.
  • Provide exceptional customer service by engaging with patrons, taking drink orders, and offering recommendations.
  • Maintain a clean and organized bar area, ensuring all equipment and supplies are in good working order.
  • Adhere to responsible alcohol service guidelines and ensure a safe and responsible drinking environment.
  • Handle cash transactions, process payments accurately, and maintain a balanced cash drawer.
  • Monitor the consumption of alcohol by guests and assist in preventing overconsumption.
  • Participate in regular inventory checks and restocking of bar supplies.
  • Collaborate with the kitchen and waitstaff to ensure seamless service and a positive overall dining experience.
  • Stay up-to-date on current drink trends, ingredients, and techniques to continuously improve and innovate our beverage offerings.
Qualifications:
  • Proven experience as a bartender in a similar establishment
  • Excellent knowledge of mixing, garnishing, and serving drinks.
  • Proficiency in using bar equipment and following established recipes.
  • Strong customer service and communication skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and high-pressure environment.
  • Knowledge of responsible alcohol service and safety practices.
  • A valid bartending license and certifications (if required by local regulations).
  • Flexibility in working evenings, weekends, and holidays.
  • Must known Cocktails:
    • Classic Cocktails Special Cocktails
    Cosmopolitan Zombie
    Margarita Mai Tai
    Mojito Whiskey sour
    Espresso Martini Lotus Martini
    Sex on the beach Midori sour
    Negroni Old Fashioned
Benefits:
  • Competitive pay and tips.
  • Opportunity for career growth within our company.
  • A fun and dynamic work environment.
  • Employee discounts on food and drinks.
  • Training and development opportunities.

If you are a talented bartender who thrives in a fast-paced, customer-focused environment and wants to be part of a team dedicated to delivering exceptional service, we invite you to apply and join the Nalu family. We look forward to having you contribute your skills and passion to our bar program.
